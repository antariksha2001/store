Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within India. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business day delivery.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for books in their original condition. Simply contact us to initiate a return.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our website.
